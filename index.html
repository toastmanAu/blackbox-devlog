BlackBox's on-device POS app takes shape on LVGL — payments, QR, full inventory CRUD and a sales cart/invoice flow on a new ILI9488 display target; the Vault companion wallet adds an on-chain Agent Gateway and embedded light client, and the board emulator goes agentic.

2026-06-15 · wave-2wallettooling

BlackBox Vault — the native Android companion wallet — ships Wave 2 with Nervos DAO, real on-chain ML-DSA-65 post-quantum sends, and biometric security gates; the board emulator gains thermal printer and QR-scanner peripherals.

Open-source ESP32 board emulator shrinks the embedded debug loop, BlackBox is being ported to LVGL on top of it, and a new post-quantum messaging protocol (cemp-pq) lands for the backend.
